Diffrentiating Padding, Border And Margin

The css box model consists of the padding, border, margin and content. The focus however is on the first three. The padding is the space between the content and the border. The border on the other hand shelters or covers the content and the padding. The margin is the outermost part,it is how much space around an object. Padding is how much space is within an object sides and its contents. Border is between the margin and padding.
